About Me

> I am Chris Kautz-Scanavy and I am training to become a junior full stack developer. I have done quite a few things since graduating college amost 19 years ago. After college I moved to Richmond as that is where my band was located and Richmond had a good music scene in the early 2000's. Playing doesn't pay the bills, so I became a beerman. Now that was fun, driving a big ass beer truck with 60 kegs plus, selling and delivering them. I mean I was the beerman... then my back said screw you, your beerman days are over. time to do something else.

I was a sub teacher briefly and this was while recovering from a back injury and a rebuilt knee. So natuarlly it was time to do outside MRO sales, nuts bolts, mechanical related stuff, and industrial chemicals. The outside sales wasn't thing that seemed like a great a fit in the MRO industry. Most of these products could be purchased locally from the a hardware, or some variation of the products. This is when I decided to join corporate American and work in downtown Richmond doing inside sales for a car dealer lead generation company. I eventually made a transition while at the same company to the Marketing dept at the same company. I have been in the online marketing industry for 14 years. That is a long time, and it is now time for me to build upon the knowledge and experience to really grow my professional skillsets and become a full stack developer. Bam! Like I dropped a mic, well not really.
